More About me

I am a dedicated and proactive Computer Science student at The City College of New York (Macaulay Honors College), where I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with an expected graduation in May 2026. My academic journey is complemented by hands-on experience in both industry and research settings. During my internship with Bank of America, I developed a project management website using HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript, which enhanced my skills in web development and design. At NASA, I engineered a remote-controlled car and integrated machine learning with Arduino, contributing to a published research article on the Geological Society of America website. Additionally, my role at ReunitedAmericaUSA involved automating website testing with Selenium and SQL database management. I have also led projects like Pix2ArtLab and am currently working on a personal portfolio to showcase my technical skills and achievements. Active in the tech community, I host Hackathon events and workshops through Beavers Code CCNY, reflecting my commitment to fostering innovation and collaboration in the field of computer science.
